Synopsis

Censured at the Council of Nikaea for the Legion’s open use of psychic sorcery, Magnus the Red withdraws with his Thousand Sons to the crystal city of Tizca on Prospero. There the primarch peers into the warp and glimpses Horus’ coming betrayal, and he hurls his mind across the void to warn the Emperor on Terra — only to crash through the half-built webway gate beneath the Imperial Palace, tearing it open to daemons.

Followed through the eyes of Captain Ahzek Ahriman and the chronicler Lemuel Gaumon, the novel charts a tragedy of brilliant minds undone by hubris and good intentions, and ends with the Wolf King’s pyres already kindling over the City of Light.

The archivist notes: paired most naturally with Prospero Burns, which walks the same years in the Wolves’ boots — the two accounts disagree, as accounts of Prospero tend to.

Studien-Notiz · Richtung 05 — Kodex

Diese Fläche: Der Riesen-Titel als Cover-Ersatz, hier in Blackletter bis 190 px — die maximal gothische Lesart von Philipps Kernidee. Rubrik-Zeile („here follows the burning of Prospero") ordnet den Titel als Manuskript-Incipit ein. Danach das Folio: Synopsis mit illuminierter Initiale (Gold-Versal im Zinnober-Rahmen — das einzige „Ornament" der Richtung, und es ist Typografie), Katalogisat als rubrizierte Marginal-Kolumne, Personenregister mit Rollen-Glossen (Antagonist in Zinnober).
