The Record

Primarch of the Thousand Sons, a peerless psyker whose pursuit of sorcery led his Legion to ruin and into the service of the Chaos God Tzeentch. His warning to Terra — hurled through the webway rather than carried by ship — broke the very wards it meant to defend, and damned the messenger with the message.
